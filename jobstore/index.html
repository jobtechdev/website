Jobstore

Today there are hundreds of apps and web services available for jobseekers in Sweden. Wouldn't it be nice if there was a place where you could find them all? We think so. Therefore we are developing an app store for jobs. We call it

Developer platform

At the same time we are building a developer platform so that even more actors can build great apps and web services offered via Jobstore. The platform contains open data, open source and some services like an open skill taxonomy. Jobstore is more than just jobs.
As an actor, Jobstore gives you a technical developing platform, data (on vacancies, candidates and more), labor market knowledge and a market place where you can offer your services. As a customer, you get the best services from a variety of actors. That makes Jobstore more attractive to jobseekers but also persons who already have a job.
We welcome every new influencer to participate.
